Committed to the Australian Pressure Care Industry and Community

Since 2003, Novis® has been providing clinical healthcare products designed for hospitals, aged care facilities, rehabilitation providers and the home care community. With collaboration from our manufacturing and distribution partners, our products and services enhance the lives of thousands of people every day.

We Are Specialists In

Complex Rehab

Our highly trained team of professionals works closely with our partners, therapists and clients to design and deliver custom solutions and we are committed to providing ongoing support and service throughout the lifespan of our high-quality equipment. Our range includes the top selling Netti 4U Tilt in space reclining wheelchair, and the TA IQ Powerchair which are suitable for indoor and outdoor use. We also range the Dietz Slimline powerchair which is ideal for compact indoor apartments and the amazing Meyra Optimus 2 powerchair which is ideal for rugged rural areas or distance travel.

Hospital

We understand that hospitals have unique needs when it comes to healthcare and rehabilitation equipment. Whether you opt for rental or purchase, we offer a comprehensive selection of mobility, sleep, and falls prevention aids, coupled with prompt delivery and dedicated support for your utmost convenience. Our range includes walkers, air pressure mattresses, wheelchairs and bathroom and daily living aids. Novis has been providing Aged Care equipment for the best clinical outcomes since 2002. We work with architects, builders, aged care facilities, and project managers to find the best solutions. Our wide range of products include the Procair alternating air mattresses, the Molift patient lifters and ceiling hoists, the Better Living nonslip floor mats and dementia support aids.

In Home Care

We are delighted to assist you with NDIS, DVA, or HCP-funded assistive technology. Our extensive range includes walkers, wheelchairs, mobility assistance devices, walking sticks, and a variety of disability aids and equipment. Whether you’re navigating the NDIS process for the first time, or you are an experienced Occupational Therapist looking to support a client, our team is here to assist you every step of the way. Navigating assistive technology can be complex, so our educators can guide you through our products, answering any technical or clinical questions you have.

Pressure Injury Prevention

Pressure injuries typically appear in the form of a sore, or ulcer, on areas of the skin that are regularly exposed to pressure. We are determined to provide 24/7 vigilant care to patients and physicians, from specialized mattresses to air cell cushions. We help you develop a better understanding of pressure injury prevention.
